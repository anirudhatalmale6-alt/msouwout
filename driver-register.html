📋 Requirements

What You Need to Get Started

Make sure you meet these basic requirements before applying.

💳

Valid ID

Government-issued identification card (CIN or passport). Must be current and not expired.

🚗

Vehicle Documents

Valid driver's license, vehicle registration, and insurance. Vehicle must be in good working condition.

✅

Clean Record

No major traffic violations or criminal record. We verify all driver backgrounds for passenger safety.

📱

Smartphone

Android or iPhone with WhatsApp and mobile data. Needed for receiving orders and live GPS tracking.
