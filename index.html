🛡 Safety First

Your Safety is Our Priority

MsouWout is built with safety at every level. Multiple layers of protection for both passengers and drivers.

👤 Passenger Safety

✓ Verified drivers with ID, license, and background check before approval
✓ See driver photo, name, and license plate before pickup
✓ Real-time GPS tracking - share your trip link with family
✓ Emergency button sends your location to contacts and MsouWout support
✓ Digital payments only - no cash means safer rides
✓ Geofenced safe zones - service only in approved, secure areas
✓ Daytime service only (6am-8pm) for maximum visibility

🚗 Driver Safety

✓ Rider identity verified before trip confirmation
✓ No cash handling - all payments digital, reducing robbery risk
✓ Zone restrictions keep you in safe, approved service areas only
✓ Emergency alert system - one tap to notify support and authorities
✓ Trip details automatically shared with your emergency contacts
✓ Route blocked if it crosses restricted or dangerous zones
✓ 24/7 support line for driver emergencies
🛡 Built for Haiti. Designed for safety. Every trip, every time.
